Vertical Antenna — NEC Modeling Reference

Structure: Vertical wire rising along Z-axis, usually with radials.
- Quarter-wave vertical: length ≈ 71.6/f(MHz) metres.
- Radials slope down from the base or lie along the ground.

Key rules:
- Vertical element: one GW from (0,0,base_z) to (0,0,top_z).
- Radials: separate GW cards extending outward in X/Y from the base, sloping down.
- Feed at the bottom of the vertical (segment 1 or last segment at base).
- GE 1 with GN 1 (perfect ground) or GN 2 (real ground).
- Use GS for unit conversion if dimensions given in feet/inches.

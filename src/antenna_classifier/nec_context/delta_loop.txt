Delta Loop Antenna — NEC Modeling Reference

Structure: Triangular full-wave loop, usually oriented vertically. Three GW
cards form the triangle sides. Circumference ≈ 1λ. Can be apex-up or apex-down,
fed at the bottom or at a corner.

Key rules:
- Three GW cards forming a closed triangle (endpoints connect).
- Oriented in a vertical plane (e.g. X-Z plane or Y-Z plane).
- Circumference = sum of wire lengths ≈ 306/f(MHz) metres.
- Bottom feed for horizontally polarised radiation.
- Corner feed for vertically polarised radiation.
- Use GE 1 with GN for ground models, or GE 0 for free-space.
- Use GS for unit conversion if dimensions in feet.

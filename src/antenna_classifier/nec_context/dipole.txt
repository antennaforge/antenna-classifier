Dipole Antenna — NEC Modeling Reference

Structure: Single horizontal wire, centre-fed, length ≈ λ/2.
The wire extends along one horizontal axis (X or Y) at a fixed Z height.

Key rules:
- One GW card (or split into 3 wires: left half, short centre, right half).
- Feed at the centre segment (centre of wire or centre short wire).
- Total length ≈ 143/f(MHz) metres for a half-wave dipole.
- Wire extends symmetrically: from −L/2 to +L/2 along one axis.
- Z coordinate is the height above ground.
- Use LD 5 for copper wire conductivity (5.8e7 S/m).

Zigzag Antenna — NEC Modeling Reference

Structure: Wire antenna with alternating zigzag bends, providing compact size
and broadband characteristics. The zigzag pattern shortens the overall span
while maintaining electrical length.

Key rules:
- Multiple GW cards tracing a zigzag path in one plane.
- Wire endpoints connect sequentially.
- Zigzag wire is longer than the direct distance — acts as a loaded antenna.
- Feed at the centre or at one end depending on the design.
- Can be oriented vertically or horizontally.
- Use LD 5 for wire conductivity.

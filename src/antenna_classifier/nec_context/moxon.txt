Moxon Rectangle Antenna — NEC Modeling Reference

Structure: Compact 2-element beam with bent-back element tips. The driven element
and reflector are folded into a rectangle shape, providing good F/B ratio in a
small footprint. Elements are horizontal with tips bent toward each other.

Key rules:
- Uses 6 GW cards: 3 for driven element (left tail, main, right tail) and
  3 for reflector (left tail, main, right tail).
- All wires at the same Z height (horizontal antenna).
- Tails bend inward (along Y axis) to couple the elements.
- The gap between tail tips is critical for performance (~0.007λ).
- Feed at centre of driven element main wire.
- Use LD 5 for wire conductivity.

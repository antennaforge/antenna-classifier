Inverted-V Antenna — NEC Modeling Reference

Structure: Dipole with ends drooping downward from a central support.
The apex is at the top (support height) and wire ends slope down at 30-45°.
Often multiband with separate wire pairs for each band.

Key rules:
- Apex (feed point) at the top: X=0, Y=0, Z=apex_height.
- Wire ends droop down and outward, ending at a lower Z.
- Each leg is one GW card from apex to end.
- For a single-band inverted-V: 2 GW cards (one per leg) + short feed wire.
- For multiband: separate wire pairs per band, all meeting at apex.
- Feed at the apex (centre, short connecting wire or junction).
- GE 1 with GN 2 for real ground models.

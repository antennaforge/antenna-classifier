Collinear Antenna — NEC Modeling Reference

Structure: Two or more half-wave elements stacked end-to-end vertically (or
horizontally), connected by phasing stubs. Provides gain over a single dipole
by extending the current distribution.

Key rules:
- Each half-wave section is a GW card.
- Phasing sections (folded stubs) connect adjacent half-waves.
- Stubs are modelled as short vertical GW pairs with a connecting wire.
- Feed at the centre of the assembly.
- Elements are collinear (aligned along the same axis).
- Use GE 1 with GN for ground models.
